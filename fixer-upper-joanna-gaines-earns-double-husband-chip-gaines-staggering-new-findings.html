    The Fixer Upper stars share five children together and often share glimpses of their lives on social media

      Joanna Gaines and her husband Chip are some of the most well-known fixer uppers in the United States, and have an impressive 18M social media followers between them. 

      What's more, new research conducted by real estate experts Agent Advice, revealed that the couple rank in the top two spots when it comes to the highest earning home improvement stars. 

      Joanna - in first place - could earn an estimated $45,329 per sponsored post on Instagram, thanks to her 13.7 million followers. 

      Chip, meanwhile, falls in second place in the findings, being able to earn $17,126 per Instagram post, reaching his 5.1 million followers. 

      The celebrity couple - best known for their series, also earn money from their Magnolia Realty, Magnolia Network, Magnolia Homes and numerous retail and restaurant businesses in their native Texas.
