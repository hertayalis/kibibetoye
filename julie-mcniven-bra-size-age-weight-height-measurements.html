    Biography:

    Julie McNiven was born on October 11, 1981 in Amherst, Massachusetts, U.S. She made her first film appearance in The Gypsy Years while she made her television debut as Suzie Waller in Law & Order: Criminal Intent.

    She has since appeared in other shows including Brotherhood, Waterfront, Desperate Housewives, Stargate: Universe, Fringe, Nikita, House and Hawaii Five-0.

    She also became known for her roles as Hildy in Mad Men and as Anna Milton in Supernatural. Her film work includes Carlito’s Way: Rise to Power, Go Go, Tales!, Across the Universe and Movie 43.
