The M.2 module, called the FG100, will use a variant of Intel’s 5G modem portfolio (it hasn’t been confirmed if this is the XMM 8160) and be targeted at gateway manufacturers such as D-Link, Arcadyan, and Gemtek who have already confirmed they will be using this module in upcoming products.
