The Benefits The primary benefit is ride quality: Vehicles with air suspension are often said to “glide” over bumps, while traditional steel spring suspension can cause a harsher ride. That’s not true of SUVs or trucks with spring suspension, where large loads can weigh down the vehicle. Similarly, it is asked, how much does it cost to bag a car? A simple air suspension involves swapping out the springs for air bags, adding a reservoir and compressor, and a control unit. The simplest systems cost around $400 U.S., while a more sophisticated set up can cost more that $1,000 — just for the parts.How long do air suspension bags last?The lifespan of the air suspension bags depends on the manufacturer, the vehicle, their system and how aggressively or conservatively they are used. Each air bag may last between 50,000 and 70,000 miles, or may need a replacement after 10 years.
