    Understanding the Buss Family Trust

    The Buss Family Trust was established by the late Dr. Jerry Buss, who was the majority owner of the Los Angeles Lakers. The trust is designed to keep the ownership of the Lakers within the Buss family, ensuring that the team remains a family-run business. The trust is managed by a board of trustees, which includes members of the Buss family.
