    Now Angelina, 37, has gone under the knife once again to get a second boob job.

    The reality star took to her Instagram profile on Friday night to share a live video from her plastic surgeon's office.

    In the clip, the TV personality sat next to her doctor while wearing a hospital gown.

    Angelina told fans: "Hi guys, so I'm actually undergoing surgery. This is Doctor Tutela he's going to tell you what we're doing. And I already tagged him, he's pinned at the bottom."

    The surgeon then chimed in to explain: "So what we're doing today is a revision of her breast lift. Angelina has lost a good amount of weight recently and it's caused her breast tissue to kinda sag over the implant, and so we're going to revise that and make it better."

    The MTV star continued: "Yes, so he's pinned to the bottom. Follow him please."

    Angelina then watched as fans flooded the comments section with questions.

    After one asked if she had lost a bunch of weight due to Ozempic, she replied: "Am I on ozempic? No I'm not. I'm not on it, I've just lost a lot of weight."

    The Staten Island native then pulled down her top to show off a bunch of black lines that had been drawn from her chest down her bust.

    "So as you guys can see, we have the road map already ready to go. Right? And so we're going to go into surgery and he will take a few pictures and he will post them so just follow him and you will see some of the videos.

    "We will not show my face, but we will show the skin It might be a little gory for some of you but I don't really care, you guys will like it."

    Angelina waited for a few more comments, and one fan asked: "You're super skinny how did you lose it all?"

    "Guys it was just stress-related, I don't really think there was a reason for it. And no it's not Ozempic cause somebody said that again. I guess it's just being on TV and I don't even know how to explain it right, like you lose weight sometimes and it is what it is, what am I going to do?" she responded.

    Directing her attention back to Doctor Tutela, she gushed: "He's the best plastic surgeon, he's done my nose, my boobs, he's doing my boobs again, cause of everything, weight loss. So love him. He's the best!"

    She captioned the post: "After unexpected weight loss due to stress, my breast started to sag again. I went to see @tutelaps for a revision breast lift with implants. While he was there I decided to upsize too.

    "Check out his story @tutelapls for intraoperative details."
