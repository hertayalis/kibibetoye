    Karla Camila Cabello Estrabao (born March 3, 1997) is a Cuban-American Singer and Songwriter from Cojímar, Havana. She rose to fame after competing in The X Factor Season 2 reality TV Show in 2012. Later, Camila became the part of the “Fifth Harmony” singing group.

    Furthermore, the group finished the show in the third place. They signed their first deal with the Syco Music (Simon Cowell and Epic Records, L.A. Reid) record label. The band released the EP “Better Together” in October 2013. Moreover, their songs “Worth It” and “Work from Home” became a huge hit in the USA.

    As a solo singer, Cabello debuted with her single “I Know What You Did Last Summer” with Shawn Mendes. Later, the song certified Platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America. With her song “Havana”, she debuted at number one on the Billboard 200 chart.

    In addition, Camila also listed in The 25 Most Influential Teens of 2016 list. Recently, the Camila’s Song Havana banned from the Spotify Ad from the United Kingdom for being too scary.

    Wiki, Age, Parents & Early Life

    The American singer was born on 3rd March 1997 in Cojímar, Havana, Cuba. Hence, the age of Camila Cabello is Twenty-one (21) years old, as of 2018. Her father “Alejandro Cabello” is Mexican and mother “Sinuhe Cabello” is Cuban. Therefore, Camila belongs to Cuban American ethnicity.

    The singer also has a younger sister named “Sofia Cabello”. In her early childhood, the star moved to Mexico City various time. Later, in 2002, her family moved to Miami, Florida, USA. Cabello attended Miami Palmetto High School. But later dropped out due to her career and studied online.

    Personal Affairs, Boyfriend & Spouse

    The singer started dating Austin Mahone (singer). On November 24, 2014, Camila confirmed that she and Austin have split up to 96.1 KISSFM. Later, she rumored to be in a relationship with guitarist Michael Clifford. The couple was spotted together at The Nice Guy restaurant.

    Furthermore, Camila later rumored to be dating Shawn Mendes. But the couple never came forward publicly about their relationship.

    Currently, she is dating her boyfriend “Matthew Hussey” (writer, dating coach, and YouTuber). They were spotted together on a holiday in Mexico.
