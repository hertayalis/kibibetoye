    Crystal Westbrooks (born March 6, 1993) is an American Television Personality, Model, Businesswoman, and Instagram Star from Florida, USA. She is famous for her appearance in the reality show “The Westbrooks” on BET Networks. Furthermore, the 25-year-old reality star appeared in the show with her all family members.

    Crystal is also extremely popular on Instagram having 1.1+ million followers. Moreover, she posted her first picture on August 30, 2013, on the image sharing application. As her popularity grew on Instagram, Westbrooks started gaining modeling contracts.

    Later, she first received her first fashion job by an online fashion retailer “Harley Dreux”. In addition, Crystal became the brand ambassadors for many companies. She modeled for brands like Tiffany Jewels, The Queen Pegasus Makeup, Slender Me Medical Health Spa etc.

    She launched her own line of products called Crystal Curls. As a businesswoman, Westbrooks products’ include shampoos, conditioners, and many other hair products.

    Profile, Age, Before Fame

    The reality TV personality was born on the 6th of March 1993 in Florida, USA. Hence, Crystal Westbrooks’ age is 25 years old, as of 2018. Her parents are Warren Westbrooks (father) and Candace Westbrooks (mother).

    Furthermore, the model has six siblings. She has two brothers named Blake and Warren Jr. The TV personality has four sisters Brooke, Morgan, Bree, and India Love Westbrooks. Crystal belongs to multiracial ethnicity.

    Westbrooks attended Ambler Avenue Elementary School in Carson California. Later, she completed her school education from Santiago High School in 2011. Moreover, the model graduated from Texas Southern University in 2016. She majored in business finance and received a bachelor degree.

    Personal Affairs, Boyfriend & Spouse

    The celebrity was in a relationship with boyfriend Drake (real name Aubrey Drake Graham). He is a famous Canadian rapper, singer, songwriter, and record producer. On April 16, 2015, the former couple was seen together at Coachella in California.

    They broke up in 2016. According to a source, her sibling India also dated Drake before Crystal. Currently, Crystal is having an affair with another rapper and songwriter, Steven Russell.
