    Ali Cobrin

    $1 million

    Ali Cobrin is an American actress. In 2009, she made her film debut in The Hole. The following year, she starred as Molly in the television series Look: The Series. That same year, she appeared in the movie Cold Cabin. She next appeared in an episode of Friends with Benefits. Later in 2012, she played the role of Kara in the comedy film American Reunion. She went on to have performances in other films, including Life’s an Itch, Neighbors, Girl House, Lap Dance, A Beautiful Now, and Outlaw. Cobrin next starred in the TV movie The Unauthorized Melrose Place Story. Her latest film role was in 2018’s The Iron Orchard.
